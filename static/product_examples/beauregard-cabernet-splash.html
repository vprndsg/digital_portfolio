Vineyard & Tasting Notes

  • Beauregard Ranch Vineyard in Bonny Doon, California.
  • Zayante sand over sandstone at 1,740 to 1,802 feet elevation.
  • Just 4.56 miles from the Pacific, giving the fruit a cooler mountain profile.
  • Native fermentation and French oak aging keep the cellar story restrained and estate-led.
  • Crushed graphite, cassis, dark cherry, cedar box, and mountain mint on the nose.
  • Fine-grained tannins with blackcurrant, espresso, and a bright coastal spine on the palate.
2021 Cabernet Sauvignon Beauregard Ranch 96 points - Vinous
