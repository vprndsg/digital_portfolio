Challenge

Many product pages describe products correctly but fail to create desire. The goal was to make the PDP do better emotional and merchandising work without burying the purchase flow.

Deliverables

  • PDP hero layout
  • Tasting notes module
  • Food pairing strip
  • Bundle recommendation
  • Mobile buy box refinement

Approach

How the project was structured.

1 / Approach

Lead with taste and shape

The product story starts with what the bottle feels like in the mouth and at the table, not just origin data and SKU details.

2 / Approach

Use modules that earn their place

Every content block either sharpens understanding, increases confidence, or creates a higher-value path like a bundle.

3 / Approach

Keep the buy box honest

The purchase controls stay visible and simple while the supporting modules do the seduction work around them.
